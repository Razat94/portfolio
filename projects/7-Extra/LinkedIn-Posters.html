A list of various background covers I made

Description

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform. Many profiles unfortunately have the following background cover image:



This image, despite it being small in size, seems bland and lifeless. With my experience in Photoshop, I decided to make a few background covers myself.

Things to note:

  • All photos are 1600 x 400.
